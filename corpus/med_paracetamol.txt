Paracetamol (also called Acetaminophen)

Use:
- Common medicine for fever and mild to moderate pain.

Dosage (for adults):
- 500â€“1000 mg every 6 hours as needed.
- Do not exceed 4000 mg in 24 hours.

Safety:
- Take with water; can be taken with or without food.
- Safe for most people when used correctly.
- Avoid combining with alcohol (risk of liver damage).

Warnings:
- Overdose can cause serious liver injury.
- Keep away from children. For children, only use correct pediatric dosing.
