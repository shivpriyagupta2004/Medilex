Stomach ache may be caused by indigestion, gas, constipation, or infection. 
Encourage the child to rest and drink warm water. 
Light meals like khichdi, rice, or bananas are easy to digest. 
Avoid junk food, soda, and spicy foods. 
Paracetamol may be given for pain relief if needed. 
If stomach ache is severe, recurrent, or associated with vomiting, fever, or blood in stool, consult a doctor immediately.
